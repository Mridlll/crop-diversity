Publication Maps: Crop Diversity & Caloric Productivity

Static choropleth maps covering 755 districts across India. Click any map to view full size.

Diversity Indices

Agro-Biodiversity Index (ABI)

Composite index combining Shannon diversity, Simpson concentration, and crop richness into a single normalized score (0–1).

Shannon Diversity Index

Measures crop diversity accounting for both richness and evenness of area allocation across crops.

Simpson Diversity Index

Probability that two randomly selected hectares grow different crops. Higher values indicate greater evenness.

Crop Richness

Total number of distinct crops cultivated in each district, reflecting breadth of cropping systems.

Combined Index Panel (2x2)

Side-by-side comparison of all four diversity indices for easy spatial comparison.
